Before you begin

What you’ll need to apply

The Architectural Review Committee can only review a complete packet — an application missing any required item is returned and automatically denied. Gather these items before you start; you’ll attach each one as you go.

Required submissions

  • Plot design of your lot, with the location of the proposed modification clearly marked. Build one with our interactive tool or upload your own.
  • Sketches, dimensions, example photos and materials for the proposed modifications.
  • Photos of your yard — answer a few questions and the form will request the photos the committee needs, with a framing example for each.
  • Signatures from adjacent property owners most affected by the change — the signature form is on the bottom of your printed packet's cover page; collect signatures in person and submit the signed form with your application.
  • Application fee — no payment is due when you apply. Once the Architectural Specialist confirms your application is complete, she will contact you to arrange payment — review does not begin until both the complete application and the fee are received.

Architectural review dates — 2026

The completed ARC packet must be submitted by the deadline to make that month’s board meeting. No exceptions. Dates subject to change.

This form prepares a complete packet based on the association’s May 2026 application. Before submitting, please confirm with the office that a packet prepared here will be accepted.

      Section checklist

        Save & print your application packet

        Opens the print view — choose Save as PDF as the printer/destination and remember where the file is saved, or print paper copies. The packet is complete on its own: page 1 is your submission instructions, with the adjacent-owner signature form on the bottom of that same page — collect those signatures in person after printing — and the application follows with your plot plan and signature included automatically. A neighbor’s signature does not constitute approval or disapproval.

        No payment is due now. Once the Architectural Specialist confirms your application is complete, she will contact you to arrange payment — review does not begin until both the complete application and the fee are received.

        Packet saved?

        This browser still holds a full copy of your application — including your signature image. Once your packet is saved and submitted, you can delete the saved draft. (Keep it if you might need to revise and resubmit.)

        What Happens Next

        1. Completeness check & fee — No payment is due when you apply. Once the Architectural Specialist confirms your application is complete, she will contact you to arrange payment — review does not begin until both the complete application and the fee are received.
        2. Committee review — The Architectural Review Committee reviews your application at the next scheduled board meeting (dates are listed under Requirements & dates). The 45-day review period begins once everything is received.
        3. Decision — If the application is approved, you’ll receive written approval. No exterior work may begin before it arrives.
