Before you begin

What you’ll need to apply

The Architectural Review Committee can only review a complete packet. Gather these items before you start — you’ll attach each one as you go. An application missing any required item is considered incomplete, and the 45-day review period does not begin until everything is provided.

Required submissions

  • Plot design of your lot, with the location of the proposed modification clearly marked. Build one with our interactive tool or upload your own.
  • Sketches, dimensions, example photos and materials for the proposed modifications.
  • Photos of your yard — we’ll ask a few questions and tell you exactly which photos to take and how to frame each one.
  • Signatures from all impacted neighbors — print the signature form, collect signatures in person, and attach the signed copy.
  • Application fee — arranged with CarolMarie Taylor after submission.

Architectural review dates — 2026

The completed ARC packet must be submitted by the deadline to make that month’s board meeting. No exceptions. Dates subject to change.

          What Happens Next

          1. Submit your application — Email the completed form and all attachments to carolmarie.taylor@fsresidential.com.
          2. Committee review — The Architectural Review Committee will review your application at the next scheduled board meeting (see dates below). The 45-day review period begins once all required materials and the fee are received.
          3. Decision & payment — If the application is approved, the committee will contact you to arrange payment of the application fee and provide written approval before any work may begin.
