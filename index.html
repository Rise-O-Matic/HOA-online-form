Before you begin

What you’ll need to apply

The Architectural Review Committee can only review a complete packet. Gather these items before you start — you’ll attach each one as you go. An application missing any required item is considered incomplete, and the 45-day review period does not begin until everything is provided.

Required submissions

  • Plot design of your lot, with the location of the proposed modification clearly marked. Build one with our interactive tool or upload your own.
  • Sketches, dimensions, example photos and materials for the proposed modifications.
  • Photos of your yard — we’ll ask a few questions and tell you exactly which photos to take and how to frame each one.
  • Signatures from all impacted neighbors — print the signature form, collect signatures in person, and attach the signed copy.
  • Application fee — nothing is due when you apply; if the application is approved, the committee will contact you to arrange payment.

Architectural review dates — 2026

The completed ARC packet must be submitted by the deadline to make that month’s board meeting. No exceptions. Dates subject to change.

      Your packet

        One item to confirm yourself:

        1

        Save & print your application packet

        Opens the print view — choose Save as PDF as the printer/destination and remember where the file is saved. Your drawn plot plan and signature are included automatically. Print a copy too: it’s the packet your neighbors review when they sign, and your record to keep.

        2

        Collect your neighbors’ signatures

        Print the signature form for the adjacent owners you listed in Section 05, collect their signatures in person — they can review the packet you printed in Step 1 — then attach the scanned or photographed form here. A neighbor’s signature does not constitute approval or disapproval.

          This step usually takes a few days — you don’t have to finish it now. Print the form, collect signatures in person, and come back: everything you’ve typed saves automatically in this browser. Just attach the scanned form in the same sitting you send your email — the browser can’t hold on to attached files between visits.
          3

          Email your packet to the committee

          Opens a pre-addressed email whose body lists every file to attach — the PDF you saved, your photos, the signed neighbor form, and any plan or sketch files. Send the scans and keep your printed packet for your records.

          Sent your email?

          This browser still holds a full copy of your application — including your signature image. Once your packet has been emailed, you can delete the saved draft. (Keep it if you might need to revise and resubmit.)

          What Happens Next

          1. Committee review — The Architectural Review Committee will review your application at the next scheduled board meeting (dates are listed under Requirements & dates). The 45-day review period begins once all required materials are received.
          2. Decision & payment — If the application is approved, the committee will contact you to arrange payment of the application fee and provide written approval before any work may begin.
